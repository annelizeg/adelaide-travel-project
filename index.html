Why Adelaide?

Your Gateway to Culture, Coast, Culinary Delights, and a World-Class Lifestyle

Nestled on the shores of the glorious Gulf St Vincent and cradled by the picturesque Adelaide Hills, Adelaide effortlessly blends a rich colonial heritage with a vibrant, modern pulse. Established in 1836 as a free settlement, avoiding the convict past of some other Australian cities, Adelaide was meticulously planned with wide boulevards, extensive parklands, and a forward-thinking vision. This thoughtful design laid the groundwork for a city renowned for its stunning architecture, accessible green spaces, and a palpable sense of calm. From its early days, Adelaide has been a hub of innovation and culture, establishing itself as a significant wine region and a city of festivals that continues to flourish.

Indeed, its exceptional quality of life has not gone unnoticed. Adelaide was recently ranked as the 9th most liveable city in the world by the Economist Intelligence Unit's (EIU) 2025 Global Liveability Index, boasting excellent healthcare, education, and infrastructure. This makes it not just an irresistible destination for every kind of explorer, but also a truly desirable place to experience a relaxed yet vibrant lifestyle.

My favorite cafes in Adelaide

Mekko Market & Cafe

Combining a cafe experience with a preloved fashion market, this Scandinavian-inspired venue offers a unique concept. It's a great spot to enjoy a coffee and a bite to eat while browsing for sustainable fashion finds.

Address:

89 Prospect Rd, Prospect SA 5082

What I like about it

I absolutely love the innovative blend of enjoying a fantastic coffee and delicious food while having the opportunity to browse unique, preloved fashion, and discovering potential hidden treasures. It's a testament to Adelaide's creative spirit and commitment to sustainability. Offering an experience unlike any other, this place is perfect for anyone looking for something a bit different!

Peter Rabbit Cafe

A charming unique garden oasis in the heart of the city that feels like a hidden world. It is known for its delicious brunch options, including cocktails, served in a vibrant and friendly environment that is consistently buzzing with happy visitors.

Address:

234-244 Hindley St, Adelaide SA 5000

What I like about it

It's like discovering a secret garden hideaway in the city. The lush, vibrant outdoor setting and whimsical atmosphere immediately puts you at ease, offering a unique escape from the urban hustle. It's the ideal place for a relaxed brunch with friends, savour the creative dishes and enjoy the buzzing yet laid-back vibe. It truly feels like a mini-vacation right in the heart of Adelaide.

My Kingdom for a Horse

A paradise for coffee lovers and foodies alike, this cafe is celebrated for its locally sourced dishes and features an on-site roastery, ensuring fresh and high-quality coffee. They also have additional locations on Pirie St and Waymouth St.

Address:

191 Wright St, Adelaide SA 5000

What I like about it

A true gem for coffee aficionados, thier dedication to roasting their own beans on-site means every cup is incredibly fresh and full of flavour. Beyond the coffee, their menu which is focused on delicious, locally sourced ingredients, to support South Australian suppliers, makes for a delightful and sustainable breakfast or lunch. It's the perfect spot to start your day with quality and comfort.
